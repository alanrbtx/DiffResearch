Cotype Nano can perform a wide range of tasks, including generating code, creating marketing content, answering customer queries, and developing training materials. It was trained using a two-stage supervised training approach (SFT), first on code and mathematical problems, and then on high-quality instructions, resulting in a benchmark score of 50.51 in the RuLLMArena evaluation.
